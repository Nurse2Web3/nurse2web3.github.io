Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 8, 2026  |  Last Updated: March 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how CodeChart ("the App"), developed by Nurse2Web3, handles information when you use our application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

⚕️ CodeChart does NOT collect, store, transmit, or process any Protected Health Information (PHI). No patient data ever leaves your device.

1. Who We Are

CodeChart is developed and operated by Nurse2Web3, a healthcare and Web3 education brand. You can contact us at nwright38@gmail.com.

2. What Information We Collect

CodeChart is designed to operate with minimal data collection. Specifically:

  • No PHI is collected. The app is a documentation tool used during emergency resuscitation events. Any information entered during use remains on your device and is never transmitted to our servers.
  • No personal data is sold. We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties for marketing or advertising purposes.
  • No accounts required. CodeChart does not require you to create an account or provide personal information to use the app.
  • Access codes. If your organization uses access codes to manage app access, those codes are validated locally and are not tied to any personal identity.

3. Voice Input

CodeChart offers an optional voice input feature to assist with documentation during time-critical events. Voice input is processed locally on your device. No audio recordings are stored or transmitted to any server by Nurse2Web3.

4. Device Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

  • Microphone — Only used for optional voice input during documentation. Not used for any other purpose.
  • Storage — Used only to allow you to export or save documentation locally on your device.

5. Data Storage

All documentation created within CodeChart is stored locally on your device. Nurse2Web3 does not have access to, and does not store, any documentation you create in the app.

6. Third-Party Services

CodeChart does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or data brokers. We do not use tracking cookies or cross-site tracking of any kind.

7. Children's Privacy

CodeChart is intended for use by licensed healthcare professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

8. HIPAA Notice

CodeChart is a documentation assistance tool. It is not a covered entity under HIPAA and is not intended to serve as a medical records system. Users are responsible for ensuring their use of the app complies with applicable healthcare regulations in their jurisdiction. Because no PHI is transmitted to or stored by Nurse2Web3, the app does not create HIPAA obligations for Nurse2Web3.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted at this URL with an updated effective date. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
