Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 11, 2026
Your data never leaves your device

CodeShield ("the App") is a private sobriety companion application designed for healthcare professionals.

Data Collection

CodeShield does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. All information entered into the app — including sobriety dates, journal entries, and AI coach conversations — is stored exclusively on your device.

Third-Party Services

The AI Coach feature uses OpenAI's API to process messages. Messages sent to the AI Coach are transmitted to OpenAI for processing according to OpenAI's privacy policy. No identifying information is attached to these requests.

Crisis resource links (988, Crisis Text Line, SAMHSA) navigate to external services. Those services have their own privacy policies.

Analytics

The App does not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13.

Contact

For questions: nurse2web3.com

Changes to This Policy

This policy may be updated periodically. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the current policy.
