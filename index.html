// About

A nurse who builds

I'm Tally — Natalia Wright, MSN, RN. I spent nearly a decade in emergency rooms, psychiatric units, and COVID-19 isolation shelters. My career was built on making the complex feel simple for people in their most overwhelmed moments.

That same instinct drove me into crypto and blockchain education. I saw an industry full of jargon, gatekeeping, and hype — and a world of beginners who just needed someone to explain it like a real person.

I'm not a coder. I'm a builder who uses AI tools to ship real products. I've launched live blockchain APIs, trading tools, and educational platforms — all as a non-technical founder. From Bedside to Blockchain isn't a tagline. It's the whole story.
